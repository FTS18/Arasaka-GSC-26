# Janrakshak GSC-26: Standard Operating Procedures

### Field Intelligence & Command Manual for Humanitarian Networks

This manual serves as the primary guidance for field coordinators and responders utilizing the Janrakshak Command Console.

---

## 1. The Janrakshak Priority Metric (JPM)

To ensure the most vulnerable survive, Janrakshak calculates a dynamic Priority Score (0-100) for every assistance request.

**Calculated Factors**:
-   **Urgency (U1-U5)**: Immediate threat to life vs. logistics support.
-   **Population Impact**: Number of people affected.
-   **Vulnerability Weight**: Extra prioritization for children, elderly, and medical cases.
-   **Time Decay**: Auto-escalation of priority for longstanding pending requests.

---

## 2. Field Operations: Incoming Intelligence

### Digitizing Paper Surveys
For field reports collected on paper:
1.  Navigate to the Requests portal.
2.  Select Upload/Scan Document.
3.  Upload a clear image of the paper survey.
4.  The system uses Gemini AI Vision to automatically transcribe and triage the request into the live dashboard.

### Situational Awareness Map
Field commanders must monitor the Live Map for clustering indicators. High-density red zones indicate an immediate need for resource relocation.

---

## 3. Volunteer Management & Trust Protocol

### Trust Score Accrual
Automated trust scores are the backbone of the dispatch system:
-   **Completion**: Points added for resolved missions.
-   **Proof of Resolution**: Mandatory image upload for mission closure.
-   **Field Reliability**: Calculated based on response time vs. assigned time.

### Mission Acceptance
Volunteers should review the AI Briefing within the mission detail page before departure. This briefing summarizes the situational risks and resource requirements extracted from the initial report.

---

## 4. Disaster Surge Protocols

### Activating Disaster Mode
Only authorized District Commanders (Admins) may toggle Disaster Mode.
-   **Action**: Immediate high-contrast UI shift for low-visibility conditions.
-   **Policy Change**: Resource matching radius is expanded, and "Life-Preservation" categories are given absolute triage precedence.

---

## 5. Security & Data Ethics

-   **Data Integrity**: Every database write is recorded in the immutable Audit Log.
-   **Privacy**: Requestor personally identifiable information is only visible to the specific volunteer assigned to the mission to protect community members.
